## Executive Summary

APP-1:10 certifies and freezes the complete **Executive Time Platform** as a stable Nexora subsystem. All phases APP-1:1 through APP-1:9 are regression-certified together. The public platform API, internal engine contracts, authority rules, and consumer contracts are frozen. No new functionality was added in this phase.

**Platform Status:** FROZEN

## Architecture Overview

```
Consumers → ExecutiveTimePlatformGateway → ExecutiveTimePlatform → Platform Resolver → Internal Engines
```

- `ExecutiveTimePlatform`
- `ExecutiveTimePlatformGateway`
- `ExecutiveTimeIntegration`
