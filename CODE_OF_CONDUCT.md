# Code of Conduct

Nexora is committed to building a welcoming, respectful, and professional community. We want contributors, users, and collaborators to feel safe participating in discussions, proposing ideas, and helping improve the project.

## Our Standards

Participants in the Nexora community are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and professionally
- provide constructive feedback
- welcome new contributors
- respect different viewpoints and experiences
- focus discussion on ideas, code, and product improvement

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or intimidation
- discrimination of any kind
- personal attacks or insulting language
- deliberate disruption of discussions or collaboration spaces
- publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for maintaining a healthy and productive community environment. This includes setting clear expectations, moderating discussions when needed, and addressing behavior that undermines respectful collaboration.

Maintainers are also responsible for applying this code of conduct fairly and consistently. The goal is to protect the community and keep project spaces constructive.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, report it to the project maintainers. Reports should include enough detail to help maintainers understand the issue and respond appropriately.

All reports will be reviewed carefully and handled respectfully. Maintainers will make a good-faith effort to protect the privacy and safety of the people involved.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action when this code of conduct is violated. Depending on the situation, actions may include:

- a warning
- temporary restriction from project spaces
- removal from project discussions, repositories, or collaboration channels

Enforcement decisions will be guided by the seriousness of the behavior and the need to maintain a respectful community environment.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is inspired by widely used open-source community standards, including the Contributor Covenant, and has been adapted for the Nexora project.
